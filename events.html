Events

our events promise engaging experiences and hands-on learning. From captivating science demonstrations to thought-provoking talks by experts.

Embrace the Community Spirit in Every Event

At the heart of our Community Science Museum lies a commitment to bringing people together through the wonders of science. Community isn't just in our name; it's the driving force behind every event we host. We believe in creating moments that not only educate and inspire but also foster connections and shared experiences. Join us in celebrating the power of community as we unfold a calendar of engaging events. From interactive workshops to family-friendly science nights, each gathering is an opportunity to learn, connect, and explore the beauty of science alongside fellow enthusiasts. Discover the synergy of knowledge and camaraderie in our upcoming events. The community is not just a part of our name; it's the essence of everything we do.

Night in the Museum

Get your family together for an exciting night in the museum as you sleep over beside dinosaurs and science displays. Bring your own sleeping bag and get ready to rough it as we go exploring the wonders of science.

Energetica Exhibition on Loan

On loan from the NEMO Science Museum in Amsterdam, the Energetica exhibition is coming to the Community Science Museum. It’s a series of installations that allow visitors to experience the power of the elements as we harness them. From solar energy powering lighting, to ‘Wind Island’ that shows how turbines are able to use and control wind to create power.

Visiting Professor of Aeronautics

It is our pleasure to announce that Prof Sheila Widnall from the Massachusetts Institute of Technology will be delivering 3 lectures on the development of aeronautics and where the future lies in this exciting ‘space’.
