Community Science Museum

Welcome to the Community Science Museum — where curiosity knows no bounds! Immerse yourself in the wonders of science with FREE admission for all. Explore interactive exhibits, join thrilling events, and ignite your passion for discovery. Let's embark on a journey of learning and fun together!

Explore the Wonders

Explore our interactive exhibits, From cosmic enigmas to intricate robotics, our diverse displays offer a unique and engaging experience for all. Whether you're a curious learner, seasoned researcher, or a science enthusiast, an extraordinary journey awaits. Join us in unraveling the universe's mysteries, making science an accessible adventure for everyone.

Engage with Family

From the marvels of physics to encounters with ancient creatures, our curated experiences cater to every member of your group. Join us in making science an adventure meant to be shared. Explore, learn, and create lasting memories in the fascinating world of our Community Science Museum. And when it's time to recharge, our onsite café offers a cozy retreat for you and your family to relax and savor delightful treats.

Events

Immerse yourself in a calendar of captivating events that go beyond the ordinary at our Community Science Museum. From thought-provoking lectures by distinguished experts to hands-on workshops for all ages, our upcoming events promise an experience that sparks curiosity and fosters a love for discovery.

Get Involved

Become an integral part of our community-driven mission by contributing your time and passion to the fascinating world of science. Whether you're interested in volunteering or seeking an enriching internship experience, there are various ways you can make a meaningful impact.

Accessibility

At the Community Science Museum, we're committed to ensuring everyone can experience the wonders of science. Our facilities are designed with accessibility in mind, featuring wheelchair ramps, audio guides, and braille display signs for the visually impaired.
