For teachers

As a community-driven museum, we are committed to working with schools to create places of learning and exploration. Our dedicated team is on hand to offer the following educational opportunities for your students:

Guided Tours:

Bring your students for enriching guided tours of the museum. Our exhibits are tailored to complement classroom learning.

Learning Laboratory:

Immerse your students in hands-on learning experiences in our state-of-the-art learning laboratory.

Video Presentations:

Access engaging video presentations crafted to excite and inspire your students about the wonders of science.

Get in Touch

Please get in touch with us to learn more about our facilities and to arrange a visit for your students. We look forward to fostering educational excellence together!
