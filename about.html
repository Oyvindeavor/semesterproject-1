About us

Discovery, education, and community engagement. learn more about us on our journey to inspire curiosity and foster a love for science in every visitor.

Our Story

At the Community Science Museum, our story is woven into the fabric of human curiosity. Established with a vision to transcend the conventional boundaries of learning, we've evolved into a haven for inquisitive minds. Our journey began with a simple belief: that science should be a shared experience, a voyage where everyone is welcome.

Diverse Exhibits, Limitless Inspiration

Step into a realm where over a thousand exhibits await your exploration. From the wonders of physics to the intricacies of biology, each exhibit is a gateway to understanding the world around us. Our commitment to hands-on learning means you're not just a spectator; you're an active participant in the fascinating journey of scientific discovery.

Igniting Passion for Science

More than a museum, we're a catalyst for sparking passion. Whether you're a student, a family, or a seasoned scientist, there's always something new to discover. We believe that the flame of curiosity, once kindled here, can illuminate a lifelong love for learning and exploration.

inclusivity in Science

In our pursuit of making science accessible, we prioritize inclusivity. The museum is designed to accommodate all visitors, ensuring that everyone, regardless of ability, can engage with the exhibits. We're committed to breaking down barriers and creating an environment where science is truly for everyone.

Beyond the Exhibits

But we're more than just a collection of exhibits. We're a community of thinkers, dreamers, and explorers. Join us for workshops, talks, and events that dive deeper into the realms of scientific knowledge. Connect with fellow enthusiasts and experts who share a passion for understanding the mysteries of our universe.

The Nightlife of Dinosaurs

And yes, about those dinosaur exhibits – a touch of humor in our narrative. While they may not come to life when the lights are on, they add a playful element to our space. It's a reminder that even in the world of science, a bit of imagination and whimsy is always welcome.

Join Us on the Journey

As you navigate our museum, you're not just a visitor; you're an explorer on a timeless quest for knowledge. Join us on this journey of discovery, where every exhibit tells a story, and every question sparks a new adventure. The Community Science Museum invites you to be a part of something extraordinary – where science meets curiosity, and exploration knows no bounds. Welcome to our world.
