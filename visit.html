Visit Us

Located at Great Russell St. Easily accessible, our museum is situated in the heart of London, inviting you to explore the marvels of our exhibits.

Guided Tours

Enhance your experience with our guided tours, departing every hour. Tours are 70 NOK per person. Our guided tours provide a personalized and informative experience, allowing you to delve deeper into the history, science, and art showcased within our walls.

As part of the tour package, each participant will receive a handy printed guide, ensuring you won't miss any of the museum's highlights. This curated guide will serve as a valuable companion, offering additional context and details about the exhibits you encounter.

Free Admission

Explore without any cost! We are thrilled to offer free admission to our museum, inviting everyone to explore the fascinating world of scientific wonders and cultural exhibits.

Acessibility

In our commitment to inclusivity, we strive to make the wonders of science accessible to everyone. Our museum is designed with the following features to ensure a welcoming and accommodating experience for all visitors:

Wheelchair Accessibility Ramps:

Navigating through our exhibits is made easy with thoughtfully designed wheelchair accessibility ramps. We believe in providing a seamless and comfortable journey for individuals with mobility challenges.

Audio Guides:

Enhance your visit with our audio guides, offering a narrated tour that provides additional insights into each exhibit. Whether you prefer to listen to detailed explanations or supplementary stories, our audio guides cater to a diverse range of learning styles.

Braille Display Signs:

For our visually impaired visitors, we have implemented braille display signs strategically placed throughout the museum. These tactile signs provide essential information, allowing everyone to engage with the displays and exhibitions independently.

At our museum, diversity is celebrated, and accessibility is a priority. We invite you to explore the world of science with us, knowing that every effort has been made to ensure an inclusive and enriching experience for all.

Food and Drink

Visit our museum's delightful café, a perfect spot to unwind and refuel during your exploration. Our café offers a diverse menu featuring Light lunches, soft drinks, coffe, snacks and more

Our café provides a cozy atmosphere for you to relax, chat, and savor delicious treats. Whether you're seeking a hearty meal or just a casual break, our café is here to enhance your museum experience. Join us for a delightful culinary journey within the walls of science and discovery.

Shop

Our shop offers a range of memorabilia from the museum as well as great gifts and activity packs that allow you to continue to explore science even after you’ve left the museum.
